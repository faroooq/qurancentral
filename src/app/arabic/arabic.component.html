Knowledge

This ARABIC LANGUAGE COURSE will provide you the knowledge to understand the Qur’an in its ORIGINAL MATN (original Arabic Text).

Eloquence

The beauty, the Eloquence, the Miracle and the Divine Wisdom of the Qur’an will penetrate the innermost depth of your heart and you will experience the joy and happiness which cannot be expressed but can only be felt in the Believer’s Heart.

Understand

How can the Qur’an guide me and how can I ponder over it and implement the Guidance of the Qur’an in my daily life if I do not understand the Language of the Qur’an!

Melodious

Listening Quran is Melodious. Basic theme of Qura'n is to establish relationship between God and His creatures. How can the relationship bonds without reciting, listening, understanding the Qura'n.

Course Material

Basic

Madina Arabic Book #1

This is a basic course. In this course you will learn basic arabic grammer and few quranic words. After completing this course, you will be able to understand 30 - 40 % of the Qura'n Inshallah.

View Course
Intermediate

Madina Arabic Book #2

This is an Intermediate course. In this course you will learn most of the arabic grammer and quranic words. After completing this course, you will be able to understand 50 - 70 % of the Qura'n Inshallah.

View Course
Advanced

Madina Arabic Book #3

This is an Advanced course. In this course you will learn the complete arabic grammer and quranic words. After completing this course, you will be able to write, understand arabic language. You will be able to speak arabic. You will be able to understand 80 - 90 % of the Qura'n Inshallah.
